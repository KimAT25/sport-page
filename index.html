Sebastian Loeb is a Nine world champion in differet rally championships.

  • 2004 - First WRC world title
  • 2005 - 2nd World Championship title WRC
  • 2006 - 3nd World Championship title WRC
  • 2007 - 4rd World Championship title WRC
  • 2008 - 5rd World Championship title WRC
  • 2009 - 6rd World Championship title WRC
  • 2010 - 7rd World Championship title WRC
  • 2011 - 8rd World Championship title WRC
  • 2012 - 9rd World Championship title WRC

Also, he had many titles in different type of races, as: WTCC, 24 Hours of Le Mans, DakaR.
